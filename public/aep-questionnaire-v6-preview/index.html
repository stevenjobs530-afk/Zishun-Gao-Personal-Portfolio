Questionnaire Draft v6

This prototype presents a small enhancement of V5 for group discussion, with clearer attention to job-search pressure, workplace connectedness, and accessible employer support. It is not the final published version and has not yet been approved through the ethics process.

Purpose Explore early-career transition challenges, job-search pressure, workplace connectedness, AI-related pressure, cross-cultural adjustment, and employer support needs.
Methods note Captures participant perceptions and does not replace employer-practice mapping, which remains a separate desk-based research activity.
Participation Participation is voluntary and anonymous. Questions can be skipped except where needed for routing.
Ethics note Literature-informed exploratory mixed-methods questionnaire, not a validated psychological scale.
Literature-informed exploratory mixed-methods questionnaire, not a validated psychological scale. V6 lightly strengthens the V5 direction by adding clearer coverage of job-search pressure, belonging and connectedness, and knowing how to access support. The data would be used only for an AEP research draft, subject to supervisor and ethics approval before any formal release. Before any formal release, this questionnaire would need to be aligned with the approved participant information sheet and consent process, including information about the research team, data handling, anonymous quote use, withdrawal rights, support contacts, and wellbeing-related sensitivity. Prototype only: no submit, no storage.

Consent and screening

These questions establish consent and route each participant to the most relevant version of the transition questions.

Consent is required to continue into the questionnaire path.

A2. Which of the following best describes your current situation?

Your answer determines whether Section B1 or Section B2 appears next.

Required

A3. What is your graduation year, or expected graduation year?

Select one option. This field is optional in the prototype.

A4. What is your current employment status?

Select one option. This field is optional in the prototype.

A5. Which industry or field are you currently in, or expecting to enter?

Select any that apply. This field is optional in the prototype.

A6. Are you currently working, applying, or expecting to work in a country/region different from where you mainly studied or grew up?

Select one option. This field helps interpret cross-cultural transition items.

A7. To what extent have AI or digital tools affected, or do you expect them to affect, your job search or early-career role?

Select one option. AI is treated as a broad workplace and recruitment issue, not only a technology-sector issue.
